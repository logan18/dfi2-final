About Cynthia

I found my passion for decorating at an early age. Rather than teen magazines my eyes were glued to the newest home décor magazines. I followed my dream to become a decorator and studied at Algonquin College. I graduated top of my class from Interior Decorating and then went on to further my studies and expanded my knowledge by earning a diploma in the Kitchen and Bath Design program. I am a hard worker and strive for nothing but the best. I can hardly wait to get my hands on my next project. Don't forget to check out my portfolio and feel free to contact me.

Specialties

I specialize in all styles of décor. I like to consider myself a chameleon because I am able to adapt to any setting. I pride my self in being able to capture everyone's style. I produce high quality sample boards. I enjoy putting together sample boards. I love being able to pull together all the thoughts in my head and put them together in a way others can share my vision.
